ENTERCOM APPLICATION DISCOVERY QUESTIONNAIRE

(Information Required for System Design & Development of MVP)

---

This document outlines the key information required to design and develop the Entercom Unified Application.

The goal is to fully understand your current operations, services, and processes so the system can be built to accurately reflect how your business works and improve efficiency.

Please provide as much detail as possible. If any section is unclear, feel free to skip and we will discuss it further.

---

1. 🏢 Products & Services

* What products do you currently sell?
  (e.g., CCTV cameras, smart doors, solar systems, etc.)

* What services do you offer?
  (e.g., installation, maintenance, repairs, inspections)

* Which services:

  * Require physical installation?
  * Require site inspection before pricing?

---

2. 🔁 Current Workflow (Very Important)

Please describe your current process step-by-step:

👉 From when a customer contacts you to when the job is completed.

Include:

* How customers reach you (calls, WhatsApp, walk-in, etc.)
* Who receives the request
* How the request is recorded
* How jobs are assigned
* How work is completed and confirmed

---

3. 👨‍🔧 Technician Operations

* Do technicians specialize in different types of work (e.g., solar, CCTV)?
* Can technicians reject assigned jobs? (Yes/No)

If yes:

* What happens when a job is rejected?
* Who is responsible for reassigning the job?

---

4. 📅 Scheduling

* How do you currently schedule jobs?
* What are your working hours?
* Can a technician handle multiple jobs per day?
* What happens if a job is delayed or not completed on time?

---

5. 💰 Pricing Structure (No Exact Prices Needed)

* How do you determine:

  * Installation cost?
  * Service/maintenance cost?

* What factors affect pricing?

  * Size of project?
  * Number of devices?
  * Distance/location?

* Do you offer packages or bundled services?

---

6. 📦 Inventory & Devices

* Do your devices already have unique IDs or serial numbers?

* Or should the system generate IDs during inventory assignment?

* How do you currently track devices and inventory?

---

7. 🔗 Existing Software & Tools

* What applications or software are currently used for:

  * Cameras?
  * Smart devices?

(We will have to review these for integrations.)

---

8. 🤖 Support & Knowledge Base

* Do you have any of the following:

  * Product manuals
  * Installation guides
  * Troubleshooting guides
  * Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

* What are the most common customer issues or complaints?

---

9. 👥 Users & Roles

Please confirm the types of users in your organization:

* Managers
* Staff (office team)
* Technicians

For each role, what actions should they be able to perform?

---

10. 🔔 Notifications

* How do you currently notify customers about updates?
  (e.g., SMS, calls, WhatsApp, email)

* What notification methods do your customers prefer? (As that will allow what method to be priotized)

---

11. 💳 Payments

* How do customers currently make payments?
  (cash, bank transfer, online, etc.)

* When should payment occur?

  * Before service (upfront)?
  * Partial payment (deposit)?
  * After service completion?

* Preferred payment platforms (if any):

  * Remita
  * Bank transfer
  * Others (please specify)

---

12. ⚠️ Current Challenges (Most Important Section)

What are the biggest challenges you currently face in your operations?

Examples:

* Delays in responding to customers
* Missed or forgotten jobs
* Difficulty tracking technicians
* Customer complaints

13. ⏱️ Response & Service Expectations

* How quickly should customer requests be responded to?
