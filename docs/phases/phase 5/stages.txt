Remaining Architectural Decisions

After reviewing all uploaded documents, only a few unresolved items remain:

1. Product Categories

Your document says:

Resolution:
Products require a Category

You should create:

Category

as a first-class entity.

2. Fulfillment Actor

Need explicit rule:

Who can mark fulfilled?

Staff only?
Manager only?
Staff + Manager?
3. Order Cancellation

Need explicit rule:

Can customer cancel before payment?

Can staff cancel before payment?

Can paid orders be cancelled?

For MVP I would document:

pending_payment -> cancellable

paid -> not cancellable

unless business wants otherwise.

4. Low Stock Behaviour

Need explicit rule:

When quantity_available <= low_stock_threshold

what happens?

For MVP:

Audit event only

No notifications yet

since notifications are Phase 6.
