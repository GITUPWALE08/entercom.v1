# Entercom — Phase: Production Stabilization After Real-User Testing

You are a Senior Full-Stack Engineer working on the Entercom platform. The platform has been running in production for several days with real users, and the customer workflow is already functioning.

## CURRENT REAL-WORLD FINDINGS

* Celery Worker and Celery Beat are healthy on Railway.
* Redis (Upstash) is healthy.
* Payment webhooks are working.
* Customer dashboard is stable.
* Notifications are clickable and deep-link correctly.
* Payment cancellation flow has been improved.
* Product availability is enforced in the UI.
* WebSocket connection attempts are being repeatedly rejected by the backend while polling remains functional.

## OBJECTIVE

Do NOT add new features. Perform production stabilization focused on internal operational workflows.

---

# PHASE A — DISABLE WEBSOCKET AUTO-CONNECT

Requirements:

* Introduce `VITE_ENABLE_WEBSOCKETS=false`.
* Prevent automatic connection attempts when disabled.
* Keep existing polling as the source of truth.
* Remove noisy reconnect loops from production logs.
* Ensure the app still receives updates through React Query polling.
